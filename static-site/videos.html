Humpback Whales Feeding 30 Yards From Beach West Cliff Dr - Santa Cruz, CA

September 15, 2015

A pair of Humpback Whales swam less than 30 yards away from waves breaking on a beach on West Cliff Dr in Santa Cruz, CA today. Hundreds of people lined the shore to watch these majestic creatures swim in these shallow waters and feed on their prey. While it isn't unusual to see sea lions and sea otters, the fact that all of these animals were traveling together indicated a bonanza of prey, under the relatively calm ocean. It was a gorgeous day filled with warmth, laughter and gentle sea creatures going about their day.
